Goal:

This site is for people looking to watch an anime to get quick results instead of having to go through
multiple pages to find info. about a certain anime. Everything can be accessed on one page like the
rating, title, image, and users can even click on the image to go right to the trailer.


Notes:

I think the most challenging part was accounting for nullable data as well as getting my
dropdown to actually work with the data. I had to reach out to the developers about
some further information to implement the API, but other than that I think it was ok. Also,
when you hover over an image it can take you to the trailer to the anime. I met the requirements
by having a searchbar, dropdown, and a generate random anime button which counts for the
3 controls, adjusting to multiple screen sizes and using a mixture of css flexbox and
grid to implement the layout as well as storing the users last search, whatever they
selected in the dropdown, and their current status of the random button. Also, I accounted for
nullable data such as an image or a trailer that was not available as well as if the user enters
nothing or something that does not exist then I show that no data was found. As for the grade I
think I would give myself and A maybe....?
